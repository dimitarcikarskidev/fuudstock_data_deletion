Child Safety Policy

Our commitment

At Fuudstock, protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) is a top priority. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward any content, behavior, or activity that involves or promotes the use of our platform by minors, or promotes sexual exploitation or abuse of minors.

We are fully committed to complying with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, and we actively cooperate with law enforcement agencies and relevant child protection organisations when necessary.
Our standards strictly prohibit
The use of our platform by minors
Any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE)
Sharing, uploading, or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
Soliciting or attempting to engage with minors
Misrepresenting age or identity
See something? Report it.

If you encounter any behavior or content that may involve the exploitation or endangerment of a child, please report it immediately by contacting our Child Safety team directly using the email below.
